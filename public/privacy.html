Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 22, 2025
Effective Date: September 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Karyakarta ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our home services platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account with us, we collect:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, address, service preferences
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Contact Information: Service addresses and contact details

Service Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our platform:

  • Booking Information: Service requests, scheduling data, service history
  • Communication Records: Messages with service providers, support interactions
  • Location Data: Service addresses and approximate location for service matching
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser information

Analytics and Performance Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve our services:

  • App Usage: Feature usage, session duration, navigation patterns
  • Performance Metrics: App performance, error reports, crash data
  • User Behavior: Anonymous interaction patterns and preferences

3. Payment Information

Important: We currently do not process in-app payments. All payment transactions are handled by third-party payment processors when payment functionality is enabled. We do not store your payment card details or financial information on our servers.

When payment functionality is enabled in the future:

  • Payment processing will be handled by secure third-party providers (e.g., Razorpay)
  • We will only store transaction references, not actual payment details
  • All payment data will be encrypted and processed according to PCI DSS standards

4. Third-Party Services

Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may use third-party services to help us understand how our app is used and to improve its performance:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics and app performance monitoring
  • Crash Reporting: Error tracking and app stability monitoring
  • Performance Monitoring: App speed and reliability metrics

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us deliver our services:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Google Cloud Platform for hosting and data storage
  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication for secure user login
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging for app notifications
  • Maps and Location: Google Maps services for location-based features

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard security practices and monitoring

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Improve our services (in anonymized form)

Account data is typically retained for 3 years after account closure, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a notice in our app

Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
